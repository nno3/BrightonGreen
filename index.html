About Us

Welcome to GreenBrighton, your ultimate guide to discovering the sustainable and natural beauty of Brighton and Hove. We're dedicated to showcasing the city's green spaces, eco-friendly attractions, and environmental initiatives.

What We Offer

  • Curated guides to Brighton's parks, gardens, and nature reserves
  • Information about sustainable and eco-friendly attractions
  • Updates on local environmental events and initiatives
  • Community-driven reviews and recommendations

Whether you're a local resident or visitor, GreenBrighton helps you explore and appreciate the natural treasures of our vibrant coastal city while promoting sustainable tourism and environmental awareness.
